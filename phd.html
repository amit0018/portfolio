Ph.D.

Earned a Ph.D. in Natural Language Processing from the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi, under the supervision of Dr. Anil Kumar Singh.

My work includes:
  • Worked on Indian Low‑resources languages and developed Machine Translation (MT) for both bilingual and multilingual systems.
  • Worked on both statistical and neural language models
  • Worked on Adversarial learning and applied reinforcement learning in Neural MT
  • Pipeline‑based Machine Translation System for Bhojpuri‑Hindi Using Modules Based on Deep Neural Network
  • Developed machine translation for Indian languages by projecting the text into the same phonetic‑orthographic space using a common encoding
  • Developed machine translation model for zero‑shot language pairs (Bhojpuri‑Hindi and Magahi‑Hindi)
  • Published several papers in reputed journals and conferences (see Publications section)
