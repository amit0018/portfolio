About Me

I am an AI and NLP researcher with over six years of research experience, specializing in generative models, multilingual processing, machine translation, and phonetic–orthographic modeling. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi. Following this, I served as a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Data Science at The University of Manchester, U.K., where I contributed to impactful cross-domain AI research.


Currently, I am an Assistant Professor at the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad, where I teach and conduct research in NLP, AI, and Machine Learning. My work bridges theoretical advancements with practical, real-world language technology applications.
